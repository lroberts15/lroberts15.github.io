Who is Lauren Roberts?

My name is Lauren Roberts I am from Murrieta CA, and I am currently studying Graphic Design at Otis Collge of Art in Design in Los Angeles. Throughout my childhood I went through that phrase were I wanted to do it all. I went from veternarian to swim instructure and artist. And even considered architect. But it wasn’t until attending Murrieta Mesa High School I began to get involved in the visual performing arts pathway were I took graphic design courses that I knew it was for me. I love being able to express myself in a create way whether it’s through typography, image-making, or photography. I love spending time with my family and friends, watching movies, going to Disneyland, laughing, loving life, cooking, and cleaning to decompress. These are just a few things that I find inspiration from.
